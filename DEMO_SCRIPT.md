# GapHunter — 3-Minute Demo Script
## Addendum I §25.2 · Word-for-Word Voiceover + Stage Directions

**Hard constraint:** 180 seconds. Zero cuts during live interactions. No transition effects during loading states. Dead air during a live scrape proves real latency. A cut proves you edited out a failure.

---

## T+0:00 – T+0:15 | Platform Introduction (15s)

**[STAGE: Browser open on live Netlify URL. Bento grid fully visible. Search panel empty. HR radar card visible in lower section. Do not click anything.]**

> "GapHunter. Real-time labor market intelligence — two audiences, one platform. Job seekers learn exactly which skills are blocking them from jobs they can win right now. HR teams see what competitors are secretly building toward. Everything is powered by live web data from Bright Data scraped at query time. Let me show you the platform working."

**[STAGE: Pause 1 second. Let the bento grid frame itself.]**

---

## T+0:15 – T+0:35 | Intentional Failure — Pre-Flight Gate Rejection (20s)

**[STAGE: Click into the role input field. Type `asdfgh` slowly — visible on screen.]**

> "Before I run a real search — watch this. I'm going to type a nonsense role."

**[STAGE: Click "Find Gaps". Wait for inline error message beneath field — appears within 1 second. Point to it. Do not clear it immediately.]**

> "That just fired exactly one Claude Haiku validation call — Gate 1 of our pre-flight system. Zero Bright Data API calls. Zero scraping cost. The gate rejected it in under a second and stopped the entire downstream pipeline. On a live platform this prevents **65 wasted API calls** per garbage input."

**[STAGE: Clear the field. Pause 2 seconds.]**

---

## T+0:35 – T+1:05 | Real Search — CV Upload + Query Submission (30s)

**[STAGE: Drag and drop a real PDF CV into the upload zone. Wait for toast: "CV parsed — [N] skills extracted."]**

> "Now the real search. CV uploaded — [N] skills extracted by Claude Haiku, raw file discarded immediately, nothing stored."

**[STAGE: Type `Data Analyst` in the role field. Set remote filter to "Remote". Click "Find Gaps". Skeleton job cards appear. Loading state visible. Do not cut.]**

> "Role: Data Analyst, United States, Remote. [click] The pipeline is now live. Bright Data SERP is discovering URLs across LinkedIn and Indeed simultaneously. 30 concurrent Claude Haiku extractions are running through a semaphore-gated concurrency limiter — capped at 10 in-flight calls to protect against API rate limits. This takes 25 to 35 seconds of real scraping. Watch the skeleton."

**[STAGE: Stay silent. Let the loading state run in full. This is the most important 20 seconds in the demo — it proves the pipeline is real.]**

---

## T+1:05 – T+1:30 | Results Render + Weighted Scoring (25s)

**[STAGE: Job cards populate. ApexCharts horizontal bar renders with top 5 gaps. Hover over the top-ranked gap bar — dbt at 87% demand score.]**

> "Results. [pause] Notice the demand ranking. dbt is first — not because it appeared most frequently, but because it appeared in the freshest, lowest-competition postings. The scoring formula is: **0.35 times frequency, plus 0.25 times freshness, plus 0.20 times opportunity score**, plus dual-source and cross-platform bonuses. Raw keyword counting would have ranked SQL first. The weighted signal surfaced the real market gap."

**[STAGE: Point to two job cards — one with 94% relevance, one with 41%. Point to their applicant counts.]**

> "Job relevance is also weighted — 40 percent is your skill match, 20 percent freshness, 15 percent competition score. The 94 percent card has 12 applicants. The 41 percent card has 340. The algorithm surfaces jobs you can actually win — not jobs that are popular."

## T+1:30 – T+1:55 | Per-Job Analysis + Optimistic Pre-fetching (25s)

**[STAGE: Click "Analyse" on the 94% match job card. Analysis panel opens. Gap breakdown visible: dbt and Airflow as gaps, SQL and Python as strengths.]**

> "Gap analysis. SQL and Python are strengths. dbt and Airflow are gaps. Application tips generated by Claude Sonnet against this specific job description. [pause] Now — the roadmap."

**[STAGE: Gesture at the populated roadmap accordion. Pause 2 full seconds before speaking. Do not fill the silence.]**

> "Zero milliseconds. [pause] The roadmap didn't load just now — it was pre-fetched the moment job cards rendered, running in the background while you were reading. The system predicted you would ask for this. That's optimistic pre-fetching absorbing 10 to 15 seconds of latency inside the natural reading window. Perceived wait time: zero."

**[STAGE: The 2-second pause after "Zero milliseconds" is non-negotiable. Do not speak into it.]**
